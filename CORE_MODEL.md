# BIM Core Model

## Philosophical Foundation
Based on Buddhist teachings:
- Four Noble Truths (Analytical diagnosis of suffering)
- Eightfold Path (Balanced practice roadmap)
- Three Baskets (Tipitaka): Sutta, Vinaya, Abhidhamma

## Core Axes
1. Ethical Axis (Sila)
2. Mental Discipline Axis (Samadhi / MBSR)
3. Wisdom Axis (Panna / Insight)

## System Structure
- Mind & Matter analysis
- Feedback and self-correction
- Balance between compassion and logic

## Application Layer
- AI alignment & ethical AI
- Management & leadership systems
- CRM, analytics, and decision support
- Daily life mindfulness practice

## Research Direction
This core model serves as a conceptual and theoretical foundation
for MBA-level applied research and potential PhD-level extensions.
